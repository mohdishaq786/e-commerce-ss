My DealMart

My DealMart Pvt is an electronic commerce website from which user can buy any products related to electronics,clothing and much more.User can pay for the product using cash on delivery as well as using Paytm.User can also track his order by using his order Id which he had received after placing the order.User has also the facility to search for any product,category as well as name etc. The service competes primarily with Amazon' s Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal.[5][6] as of March 2017, My DealMart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.[7] My DealMart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.[8] My DealMart also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). In August 2018, U.S.-based retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in My DealMart for US$16 billion, valuing it at $22 billion.
